# Marketing Assets & Theme Package

## Feature List

### Headline
**Modern Theme** - A world-class Shopify theme that converts browsers into buyers.

### Tagline
Beautiful. Fast. Conversion-Optimized. Built for modern e-commerce.

### Key Features (Copy-Ready)

1. **4 Style Presets** - Minimal Clean, Bold & Modern, Luxury Elegance, or Playful & Vibrant. One-click transformation.

2. **Dark Mode Built-In** - Automatic dark mode that respects system preferences. Toggle in header.

3. **25+ Custom Sections** - Hero, testimonials, FAQ, countdown, Instagram, video, gamification, urgency bars, and a 15-block page builder.

4. **Conversion Machine** - Urgency countdown, low stock warnings, social proof popups, free shipping progress, trust signals, and smart product recommendations.

5. **Blazing Fast** - 90+ Lighthouse scores with critical CSS, intelligent lazy loading, link prefetching, and a service worker for offline support.

6. **WCAG 2.1 AA Accessible** - Skip links, focus traps, screen reader support, keyboard navigation, and touch target compliance.

7. **Mobile-First** - Touch gestures, swipe navigation, haptic feedback, pull-to-refresh, and sticky add-to-cart bar.

8. **Gamification** - Points system, achievement badges, streak counters, and spin-the-wheel discounts to boost engagement.

9. **AI-Powered Search** - Fuzzy matching with typo tolerance, auto-suggest, search history, and price drop alerts.

10. **Multi-Language Ready** - Full RTL support for Arabic/Hebrew, locale-aware formatting, and Shopify translation integration.
